Applications & Industries

Product and application mapping for industrial water, gas treatment, purification, and process support.

Water Treatment

Municipal and industrial systems using PAC, alum, ferric coagulants, flocculants, ion exchange resins, and specialty media for clarity and hardness control.

Gas Treatment & Drying

Molecular sieve and activated alumina products for compressed air, natural gas dehydration, solvent drying, and PSA systems.

Refinery & Petrochemical

Hydrocarbon separation, gas polishing, catalyst support, tower packing, and process media support for high-throughput operations.

Pharmaceutical & FMCG

Clean, documented adsorbents and media for process purity, packaging dryness, decolorization, and moisture protection.

Power & Utility

Demineralization, boiler feed preparation, cooling tower stability, and scale prevention using ion exchange and antiscalant systems.

Food & Beverage

Bleaching earth, activated carbon, and specialty media for decolorization and product purification requirements.
