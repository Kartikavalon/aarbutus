Molecular Sieve 3A

A 3A molecular sieve is used where only water must be removed while avoiding co-adsorption of larger molecules.

Request TDS / CoA / Quote
Applications
Solvent drying, air dehydration, cracked gas polishing
Typical Specifications
Aperture 3Å; high crush strength; low water uptake
Grades / Forms
Beads, pellets
Technical Notes
Preferred for drying of solvents and compressed air; regeneration conditions depend on operating temperature and cycle design.
Documentation
CoA, TDS, MSDS available on request
Recommended Use
Use where molecular size selective dehydration is required with low hydrocarbon loss.
